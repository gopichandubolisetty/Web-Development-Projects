My top 3 movies of all time.


Jersey

Jersey is a 2019 Indian Telugu-language sports drama film written and directed by Gowtham Tinnanuri and is produced by Suryadevara Naga Vamsi under his production banner Sithara Entertainments. The film stars Nani and Shraddha Srinath in the lead roles while Harish Kalyan, Sanusha, Sathyaraj, Sampath Raj, and Viswant Duddumpudi play pivotal roles. The film's music was composed by Anirudh Ravichander, with cinematography handled by Sanu John Varughese and editing done by Naveen Nooli.
